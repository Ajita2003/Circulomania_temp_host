LCA Features

Resource

Material, energy, or other input that is utilized in the production

Processing

Various activities and transformations that materials and resources undergo during the production

Packaging

Materials used to enclose, protect, and present products

Energy

Consumption of various forms of energy throughout the entire life cycle of a product, process, or service

Distribution

Transportation and logistics activities involved in moving products or materials from one location to another

End of Life

Final stage of a product's life cycle, where the product is no longer in use and its disposal or recovery is being considered
